What Is VibeDrop?

VibeDrop is a social music-sharing web app where you and your friends can exchange songs anonymously through private groups called Sound Circles.

At each group's scheduled Drop Time, the submitted song list is finalized and can be pushed directly to each user's Spotify account. Members then return to VibeDrop to give feedback on the songs, helping unlock powerful new stats like Drop Cred and Vibe Index in future updates.

Whether youâ€™re trying to flex your taste or just discover bangers from your friends, VibeDrop turns music sharing into a weekly ritual.

How to Use VibeDrop

  1. Create an account and connect your Spotify
  2. Join or create a Sound Circle
  3. Submit one song before the weekly (or daily) Drop Time
  4. Once the Drop happens, hit the Create Playlist button to create the playlist on your Spotify
