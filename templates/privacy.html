Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 20, 2025

VibeDrop (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website and connect your Spotify account.

1. Information We Collect

  • Spotify Account Information: Your Spotify username and authentication tokens, provided via Spotify’s secure OAuth login.
  • Submissions & Feedback: Songs you submit to Sound Circles and the feedback (likes/dislikes) you provide.
  • Basic Account Info: Your VibeDrop username, email (if provided), and Sound Circle memberships.

Note: We do not collect or store your Spotify password.

2. How We Use Information

  • Create and manage Sound Circles
  • Generate and update shared playlists on Spotify
  • Calculate Drop Cred and Vibe Index
  • Improve the VibeDrop experience

We will never sell or share your personal data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Retention

  • Submissions and feedback are stored for scoring and feature functionality.
  • Old data may be deleted or archived when no longer needed.
  • You can request account deletion anytime.

4. Sharing of Information

Your submissions and feedback are visible only within your Sound Circles. If you make your account public, some scores like Drop Cred may be visible to others.

5. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including:

  • Secure Spotify OAuth authentication
  • Database protections
  • Planned use of CSRF tokens and other web security best practices

No system is 100% secure. By using VibeDrop, you accept this risk.

6. Your Rights

  • Disconnect your Spotify account anytime
  • Delete your VibeDrop account
  • Contact us with questions: support@vibedrop.app

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted here with a new "Last Updated" date.
